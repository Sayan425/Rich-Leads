The Rich Leads Compliance Charter

We operate under a strict framework designed to protect privacy while empowering businesses with legitimate intelligence.

1. No Personal Data Harvesting

We do not scrape, store, or sell private social media profiles or non-business personal contact information. Our data is focused on publicly declared business identities and official corporate filings.

2. DPDP Act Alignment

We have proactively aligned our data processing workflows with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act of India. We only process data for legitimate business interests where a clear professional context exists.

3. Legitimate Sourcing

Our intelligence is derived from over 50 public and proprietary sources, including statutory registers, official corporate announcements, and high-trust business directories.

4. Right to Opt-Out

As part of our commitment to transparency, any professional can request to have their business profile reviewed or removed from our directory by contacting our compliance officer.

GDPR Ready

For Indian enterprises with global operations, we ensure that our data usage and transfer protocols meet the stringent requirements of the EU/UK GDPR.

Security Standards

We utilize enterprise-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit, ensuring that your research remains private and secure.

Message for Compliance Officers

Rich Leads provides full transparency on data lineage. We are happy to participate in your vendor risk assessment process.
