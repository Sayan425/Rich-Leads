Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

1. Is this a subscription or a one-time purchase? +
All Rich Leads plans are one-time purchases. You pay once and get access to the data included in your selected plan—there are no monthly or recurring fees.
2. How are the pricing tiers different from each other? +
Plans are differentiated by data coverage and filtering depth. Higher tiers provide access to more companies, more director records, and additional filters such as revenue and profitability.
3. Can I see the data before purchasing a plan? +
Yes. You can request a free decision-maker lead list based on your targeting criteria to understand the data structure and relevance before buying.
4. Does the data include both email IDs and phone numbers? +
Yes. All plans include director-level email IDs and phone numbers, allowing teams to run email, calling, and multi-channel outreach.
5. Can my sales and marketing teams use the same data? +
Yes. The data can be shared internally across your sales and marketing teams for outreach and campaign planning.
6. Do you offer custom datasets or larger coverage? +
Yes. If you need access beyond the listed plans or require specific industry or region coverage, you can contact our team for a custom data package.
