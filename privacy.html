Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Rich Leads ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals while providing essential business intelligence to our clients. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of professional business information.

2. Information We Collect

Our database contains professional business information related to Indian companies and their management. This includes:

  • Official company names and registration details.
  • Professional business titles and roles.
  • Statutory filing information.
  • Publicly available business contact signals.

Note: We do not collect "sensitive personal data" as defined under Indian law.

3. Purpose of Processing

We process information for legitimate business purposes, including helping our clients identify potential B2B partners, perform corporate due diligence, and conduct professional market research.

4. Data Sourcing

Our data is collected from publicly available sources, including corporate registers, financial reports, and professional business directories. We utilize proprietary technology to normalize and verify this data.

5. Your Rights & Opt-Out

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, you have rights regarding your data. If you wish to review, correct, or request the removal of your professional profile from our directory, please email privacy@richleads.in.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our data and ensure that it is used only by authorized business users.
